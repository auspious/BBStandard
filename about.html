Who We Are

Zimbabwe's First SECZ-Approved Crowdfunding Platform

Crowdaxe Capital (Private) Limited is a technology-enabled, regulated marketplace designed to bridge Zimbabwe's structural private capital gap. We connect growth-stage businesses with a broad base of sophisticated investors through three standardised investment structures.

Approved for sandbox testing by the Securities and Exchange Commission of Zimbabwe (SECZ), Crowdaxe operates under full regulatory oversight with mandatory KYC/AML compliance, a segregated Investor Protection Fund, and standardised fundraising terms across all structures.

Market Context

Zimbabwe's Private Capital Gap

Zimbabwe's capital markets are characterised by limited access to formal financing for SMEs and growth-stage projects. Crowdaxe addresses this structural gap through a technology-enabled, regulated marketplace that reduces transaction costs and broadens investor access.
