Investor Protection

Investor Protection Scheme (IPS)

A dedicated financial mechanism designed to enhance investor confidence, mitigate platform-level risks, and promote sustainable growth across the crowdfunding ecosystem.

Purpose

To enhance investor confidence, mitigate platform-level risks, and promote sustainable growth by establishing clear governance protocols, mandatory issuer due diligence, and a dedicated financial backstop for qualifying adverse events.

The Need

Crowdfunding investments are inherently speculative and often illiquid, with risks of total loss. The IPS provides an additional layer of oversight and a defined mechanism for addressing certain material issuer failures and platform accountability.

Key Mechanism

Funded through a Protection Fund, capitalised by a 1% IPS levy on platform fees and governed by an independent trustee. Resources are used exclusively for investor compensation in predefined scenarios such as proven material fraud.

Strategic Benefit

The IPS differentiates Crowdaxe as a responsible finance platform. It signals commitment to integrity, transparency, and the long-term health of the private capital markets the platform facilitates.

Platform Markets

Primary & Secondary Market Structure

Crowdaxe provides two distinct marketplaces, facilitating both initial investment placement and subsequent liquidity through a structured secondary market.

Issuer Application

SECZ-registered issuers apply to raise capital through the platform with mandatory documentation and due diligence.

Primary Market

Initial public offering by placement through Introduction to the marketplace. Investors subscribe to new positions.

Secondary Market

Exit mechanism for investors seeking to exit a position. Other investors can acquire existing positions through structured transfer.

Investing in private companies involves risks, including illiquidity, lack of dividends, loss of investment, and dilution, and it should be done only as part of a diversified portfolio.

Crowdaxe is targeted exclusively at investors who are sufficiently sophisticated to understand these risks and make their own investment decisions.
