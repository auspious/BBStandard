Investor Protection

Investor Protection Scheme

A dedicated financial mechanism designed to enhance investor confidence and mitigate platform-level risks within the crowdfunding ecosystem.

Purpose

The IPS establishes governance protocols, mandatory issuer due diligence, and a dedicated financial backstop for qualifying adverse events. It is designed to enhance investor confidence and promote sustainable growth across the platform.

Funding Mechanism

The Protection Fund is capitalised by a 1% IPS levy applied to all funds raised through the platform. All levy funds are held in a segregated account, separate from Crowdaxe operating accounts, and administered by an independent trustee.

Coverage Scope

The IPS provides compensation in predefined scenarios including proven material fraud by an issuer. Coverage terms, limits, and claims procedures are set out in the IPS Framework Document, available to registered investors. The IPS does not cover general investment losses, market risk, or issuer commercial failure.

Governance

The Protection Fund is governed by an independent trustee with fiduciary responsibility for fund administration, claim adjudication, and reporting. The trustee operates independently of Crowdaxe Capital management.

Important Notice

Crowdfunding investments are inherently speculative and often illiquid, with the risk of total capital loss. The IPS provides an additional layer of oversight but does not guarantee the return of invested capital. Investors should not rely on the IPS as the primary form of investment protection.

Platform Markets

Primary & Secondary Market Structure

Crowdaxe operates two distinct marketplaces: a primary market for initial investment placement and a secondary market for liquidity through structured position transfer.

01

Issuer Application

SECZ-registered issuers apply to raise capital through the platform with mandatory documentation and due diligence review.

02

Primary Market

Approved issuers are introduced to the marketplace. Investors subscribe to new positions during the fundraising cycle.

03

Secondary Market

Existing investors may list positions for transfer. Qualified investors can acquire existing positions through structured settlement.

Risk Disclosure

Investing in private companies and unlisted securities involves substantial risks, including illiquidity, lack of dividends, potential loss of the entire investment, and dilution. These investments should only form part of a diversified portfolio.

Crowdaxe is targeted exclusively at investors who are sufficiently sophisticated to understand these risks and make their own investment decisions.
You will only be able to invest via Crowdaxe once you are registered as sufficiently sophisticated under the applicable SECZ framework.
Investment opportunities presented on the platform are not offers to the public. Investments can only be made by registered members of Crowdaxe.
Past performance is not a reliable indicator of future results.
