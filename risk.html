Risk Disclosure

Understanding Investment Risk

Investing in private companies and unlisted securities involves substantial risks. Read this disclosure in full before registering as an investor.

Risk Disclosure Statement

Investing in private companies and unlisted securities involves substantial risks, including illiquidity, lack of dividends, potential loss of the entire investment, and dilution. These investments should only form part of a diversified portfolio.

Crowdaxe is targeted exclusively at investors who are sufficiently sophisticated to understand these risks and make their own investment decisions. You will only be able to invest via Crowdaxe once you are registered as sufficiently sophisticated under the applicable SECZ regulatory framework.
Investment opportunities presented on the platform are not offers to the general public. Investments can only be made by registered members of Crowdaxe Capital who have completed the full onboarding and sophistication assessment process.
Unlisted securities are illiquid. You may not be able to sell or transfer your investment easily or at all. Even where a secondary market is available on the platform, there is no guarantee that a buyer will be found or that any position can be transferred at a price equal to or greater than the original investment amount.
Businesses that raise capital through Crowdaxe are early-stage or growth-stage companies. The failure rate for such businesses is high. You should be prepared for the possibility of losing your entire invested capital.
Past performance is not a reliable indicator of future results. Any projections or forward-looking statements made by issuers on the platform are speculative and should not be relied upon as predictions of actual outcomes.
The Investor Protection Scheme provides a financial backstop for defined adverse events (specifically proven material issuer fraud) but does not cover general investment losses, market risk, or issuer commercial failure. The IPS is not an insurance product or guarantee of capital return.
Crowdaxe Capital (Private) Limited has been approved for sandbox testing by the Securities and Exchange Commission of Zimbabwe (SECZ). Crowdaxe does not provide investment, tax, or legal advice. Seek independent professional advice as required before making any investment decision.

Capital at Risk. Investing in unlisted securities involves risks including illiquidity and potential total loss of capital. These investments are not covered by the Deposit Protection Scheme. Seek independent advice where appropriate.
