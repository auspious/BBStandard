Investor Onboarding Process

All investors must complete the regulated onboarding process before accessing investment opportunities. Registration is subject to identity verification and sophistication assessment.

1

Register

Create an account with personal and contact details.

2

Identity Verification

Submit identity documentation and confirm eligibility (18+).

3

Sophistication Assessment

Complete the investor sophistication assessment as required by SECZ regulations.

4

Terms & Risk Acknowledgement

Review Terms & Conditions and formally acknowledge investment risks.

5

Platform Access

Access the investment marketplace as a registered sophisticated investor.

Capital at Risk. Investing in unlisted securities involves risks including illiquidity and potential total loss of capital. Seek independent advice where appropriate.
